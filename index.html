Who We Are

Tailored Housing for
Corporate Needs

Elite Host Services provides bespoke corporate housing solutions for businesses, HR departments, and project leads seeking comfort, reliability, and exceptional service across the Greater Toronto Area.

From employee relocations and temporary project assignments to executive stays and team accommodations, our fully managed residences are designed to deliver a seamless living experience with the professionalism today's businesses demand.

Our dedicated corporate team works directly with HR departments, relocation managers, and project leads to ensure every placement meets the highest standards of comfort and professionalism — from day one through departure.

Simple Process

How It Works

01

Contact Our Team

Reach out by phone or email to discuss your housing requirements, travel dates, group size, and any specific needs. Our team responds within the hour.

02

Select & Confirm

Choose from our portfolio of premium residences. We handle consolidated invoicing, flexible lease terms, and all booking arrangements directly with your team.

03

Move In & Relax

Self check-in via smartlock on your schedule. Your residence is professionally cleaned, fully stocked, and ready from day one — with 24/7 support throughout your stay.
